- **Colors:** Clean white background, blue links
- **Fonts:** Libre Baskerville (serif) + Source Sans 3
- **Vibe:** Academic, intellectual, reading-focused

## Customization

Edit `index.html` to change:
- Your name and bio
- Essays and writing samples
- Research publications
- Contact information

Edit `styles.css` to modify:
- Colors (light or dark theme)
- Fonts
- Spacing and layout
