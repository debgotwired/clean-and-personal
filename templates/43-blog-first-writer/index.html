About Emma

I'm a writer and researcher exploring the intersection of technology, culture, and human behavior. My work has appeared in The Atlantic, Wired, and The New York Times Magazine.

For the past decade, I've been investigating how digital systems shape our lives, relationships, and societies. I believe in slow, thoughtful writing that helps us make sense of rapid change.

I hold a PhD in Media Studies from MIT and previously worked as a research fellow at Harvard's Berkman Klein Center for Internet & Society.
