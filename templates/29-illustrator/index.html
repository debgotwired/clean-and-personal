About

Hi, I'm Aria

I'm a freelance illustrator based in Portland, creating vibrant, story-driven artwork for books, magazines, brands, and personal projects. My work blends traditional and digital techniques to bring characters and concepts to life.

With 8+ years of experience, I've collaborated with publishers, startups, and creative agencies worldwide. I believe every illustration should tell a story and evoke emotion.
